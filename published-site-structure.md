## Services Page (/services)
Cards with images for each service:
1. Immigration Bonds (POPULAR) - Secure release from detention through bond hearings
2. Removal Defense (POPULAR) - Fight deportation proceedings in immigration court
3. Citizenship & Naturalization - Become a U.S. citizen through naturalization
4. Family-Based Immigration (POPULAR) - Reunite with loved ones through family petitions
5. Humanitarian Relief - Asylum, VAWA, U visas, and protection from persecution
6. Employment Immigration - Work visas and employment-based green cards
7. Cancellation of Removal - Stop deportation for long-term U.S. residents
8. ICE Detention Representation (POPULAR) - Legal representation for detained individuals
